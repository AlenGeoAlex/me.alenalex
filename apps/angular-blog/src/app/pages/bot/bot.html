BloggiBot

Link preview crawler for Alen's Blog (EditorJS)

What does it do?

BloggiBot fetches link metadata (title, description, image) when I creates a new post and want to cite/link your page, this happens only when I am writing up a post and pastes a URL into the blog editor. It respects robots.txt and only fetches pages on demand — it does not crawl or index your site. Its one time call and will be stored in along with the post content.

User-Agent

BloggiBot/1.0 (+https://alenalex.me/bot)

Block BloggiBot

Add the following to your robots.txt to block BloggiBot from your domain: 

User-agent: BloggiBot
  Disallow: /

Alternatively, email {{'contact@alenalex.me'}} and I will manually add your domain to the blocklist.


Frequency

BloggiBot only fetches a URL once when an author pastes it into the editor. It does not periodically re-crawl or index your content. It caches the metadata for a URL for 24 hours. It will be stored in along with the post content.


Open Source

The entire code for this is open source and transparent. You can check it out at Github.
